> **+lead** — Manage your active Leads

**Usage**
```
+lead/list
+lead/clue <id>
+lead/convert <id>    (staff only)
```

Leads are generated by +investigate. Each Lead needs a certain number of clues before it can be converted into a Dungeon Contract.

**Switches**
- `/list` — Show all your open leads with their clue progress.
- `/clue <id>` — Mark one clue on a lead. Automatically converts to a Contract when the threshold is reached.
- `/convert <id>` — Staff only. Force-convert a lead without gathering all clues.

**See also:** +investigate, +contract
