> **+carouse** — Roll to revel in the Tavern

**Usage**
```
+carouse
```

Requires an active Tavern Night in your room (opened from the scene play menu or by staff).

Rolls 2d6 + Heart. Gimmick bonuses apply if relevant.

**Results**
- **10+** Clear 2 Stress and gain 2 Vibe.
- **7-9** Clear 1 Stress, gain 1 Vibe — but you have to make a choice.
- **6-** Tavern Fallout. Mark XP.

**See also:** +imbibe, +sober, heroesguild-tavern-hud
