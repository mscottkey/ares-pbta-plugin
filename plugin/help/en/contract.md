> **+contract** — View and accept Dungeon Contracts

**Usage**
```
+contract/list
+contract/take <id>
+contract/post <title>=<desc>    (staff only)
```

Contracts are the missions of the Heroes Guild. Each has a random Modifier that changes the conditions of the dungeon.

**Switches**
- `/list` — Show all available (posted) contracts.
- `/take <id>` — Accept a contract and open a jobs ticket. Creates an active Dungeon Run in your current room.
- `/post <title>=<desc>` — Staff only. Post a new contract with a random modifier.

**Modifiers** are drawn randomly from: Cursed Gold, Anti-Magic Field, Haunted, Flooded Lower Levels, Boss Guarded, Trapped Entrance.

**See also:** +dungeon, +investigate, +lead
