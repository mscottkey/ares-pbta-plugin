> **+investigate** — Dig for information and generate Leads

**Usage**
```
+investigate
```

Rolls 2d6 + Cunning. On a hit, generates a PbtaLead — a rumor or clue that can eventually be converted into a Dungeon Contract.

**Results**
- **10+** Strong hit — Lead requires only 2 clues to convert.
- **7-9** Weak hit — Lead requires 4 clues. You also drew unwanted attention.
- **6-** Miss — Mark XP. Doom advances if a Dungeon Run is active.

Leads are tracked per-character. Add clues with `+lead/clue <id>` or investigate from the Tavern HUD.

**See also:** +lead, +contract
