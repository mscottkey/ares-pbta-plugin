> **+advance** - Spend XP to advance your character

**Usage**
```
+advance/stat <stat>
+advance/move <move name>
```

Requires 10 XP (earned by rolling misses). On advance, XP resets to 0.

**Switches**
- `/stat` - Raise a stat by +1. Maximum +3. Valid stats: brawn, cunning, flow, heart, luck.
- `/move` - Learn a new move from the universal or role-specific move list.

**Examples**
```
+advance/stat brawn
+advance/move Vanish
```

**See also:** +sheet, +move
