> **Playbook Setup** — Chargen stage

This stage initialises your Heroes Guild character.

**Step 1 — Auto-Populate Stats**

Click "Auto-Populate Playbook Stats" to copy your Playbook's starting stats from the Groups tab. You must have selected a Playbook group before doing this.

**Step 2 — Choose a Gimmick**

Your Gimmick is your signature quirk — one mechanical bonus that defines your style. Choose one from the list. You cannot change it after chargen without staff assistance.

**Gimmicks**
- **The Drunk** — +1 Luck when in the Tavern.
- **Dungeon Veteran** — +1 to Defy Danger when Doom is 7 or higher.
- **Guild Rat** — +1 Heart for Investigate moves.

Both steps are required to pass the application review.
